Popular Foods

Linidkid

A traditional delicacy made from grated cassava (kamoteng kahoy), Linidkid is a staple snack in Tudela. The cassava is mixed with coconut milk and sugar, then wrapped in banana leaves and steamed to perfection, resulting in a sweet and hearty treat.

Pineapple Pie

Tudela is renowned for its delectable pineapple pie, featuring a golden, flaky crust filled with sweet and tangy pineapple filling. This delicacy showcases the abundance of locally grown pineapples and is a must-try for visitors seeking a taste of the tropics.

Puto Biko

This native delicacy combines glutinous rice cooked in coconut milk with brown sugar, resulting in a sweet and sticky rice cake. Topped with latik, Puto Biko is a popular treat during festivals and special occasions in Tudela.

Binaki

A unique delicacy made from grated young corn mixed with milk and sugar, then wrapped in corn husks and steamed. Binaki offers a sweet and moist corn-flavored treat, reflecting the agricultural abundance of Tudela.

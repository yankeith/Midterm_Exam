What is the Municipality of Tudela?

Escape to the serene coastal town of Tudela, where breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality await you. Nestled between the pristine waters of Iligan Bay and the lush greenery of Mount Malindang, Tudela offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. Immerse yourself in the town’s vibrant Binalbal Festival, where colorful masks and lively parades bring the streets to life every New Year’s Day.

Indulge in local delicacies like the famous Pineapple Pie and Linidkid, made from the freshest homegrown ingredients. Whether you're exploring its eco-tourism sites, enjoying fresh seafood by the bay, or experiencing the warmth of its people, Tudela promises an unforgettable getaway.

Popular Event

1. Binalbal Festival

The most prominent event in Tudela is the Binalbal Festival, celebrated annually on January 1st. This unique festival serves as a New Year's celebration where participants don elaborate masks and costumes, transforming into various characters, including supernatural beings like witches and ogres. The term "Binalbal" is derived from the local word "balbal," referring to such mythical creatures. The festival features a grand parade, street dancing, and a masquerade ball, fostering a sense of unity and joy among locals and visitors alike.

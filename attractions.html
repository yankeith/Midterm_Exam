Popular Attractions

1. Subanen Village

The Subanen Village in Tudela, Misamis Occidental, is a cultural heritage site and eco-tourism destination that showcases the rich traditions and way of life of the Subanen people, the indigenous group of northwestern Mindanao.

2. Vista Ella

Mines View Park offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. You can enjoy shopping for local souvenirs and take pictures in traditional Cordilleran attire.

3. Tonggo River

The Tonggo River, is a picturesque waterway located in Sitio Tonggo, Barangay Namut, within the municipality of Tudela, Misamis Occidental, Philippines. This river is renowned for its clear, refreshing waters and the lush greenery that surrounds it, making it a favored spot for both locals and tourists seeking a natural retreat.

4. Blue Lagoon

The Blue Lagoon, also known as the Crystal Blue Lagoon, is a hidden gem located in Sitio Tonggo, Barangay Namut, within the municipality of Tudela, Misamis Occidental, Philippines. This natural attraction is celebrated for its clear, azure waters and the serene environment that envelops it, making it an ideal spot for visitors seeking tranquility and a close connection with nature.
